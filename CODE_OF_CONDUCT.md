# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Helping others learn

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or hateful language
- Trolling, intimidation, or doxxing
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other
contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
